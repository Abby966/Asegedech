Who We Are

Asegedech Asfaw Aged Person Aid and Rehabilitation Organization is a nonprofit established in 1990 in Dire Dawa, Ethiopia, in Wro. Asegedech’s personal home. Initially, Wro. Asegedech cared for 6 elders, providing basic necessities like food, shelter, and clothing.

This inception won the hearts of her family and friends, leading to the creation of a wider-scale local nongovernmental aid organization for the aged.

Mrs. Asegedech Asfaw dedicated herself to building this organization. Her ultimate desire was met through her hard work. It’s time for individuals like you to get involved to expand our services and increase the number of beneficiaries.

Since its founding, the organization has grown significantly, now supporting over 91 elders with comprehensive care, including shelter, food, and medical aid. Our mission is to honor the elderly by providing them with dignity, care, and a sense of community, recognizing their invaluable contributions to society.

Over the years, we have expanded our programs to include recreational activities, health check-ups, and community engagement initiatives, fostering a holistic approach to elder care. Our dedicated staff and volunteers work tirelessly to ensure that each beneficiary feels valued and supported.

We believe in the power of community involvement. By partnering with local organizations, donors, and volunteers, we aim to scale our impact and reach more elders in need across Dire Dawa and beyond. Join us in our mission to create a brighter future for the elderly.
