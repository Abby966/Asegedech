Who We Are

Asegedech Asfaw Aged Person Aid and Rehabilitation Organization is a nonprofit established in 1990 in Dire Dawa, Ethiopia, in Wro. Asegedech’s personal home. Initially, Wro. Asegedech cared for 6 elders, providing basic necessities like food, shelter, and clothing.

This inception won the hearts of her family and friends, leading to the creation of a wider-scale local nongovernmental aid organization for the aged.

More

Mrs. Asegedech Asfaw dedicated herself to building this organization. Her ultimate desire was met through her hard work. It’s time for individuals like you to get involved to expand our services and increase the number of beneficiaries.
