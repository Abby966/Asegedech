Our Services

Shelter Aid

In our facility, 91 elders (66 male and 25 female) are currently getting full shelter and clothing aid. These beneficiaries are also provided with personal cleaning materials.

Our shelter program ensures a safe and comfortable living environment, with regular maintenance and upgrades to our facilities. We provide bedding, clothing, and hygiene kits, tailored to the needs of each elder, fostering dignity and well-being.

Food Aid

The food aid program provides three meals per day for 91 inmates (66 male and 25 female) and additional 20 elders (17 male and 3 female) living outside the facility.

Our meals are nutritionally balanced, prepared with fresh ingredients, and designed to meet the dietary needs of the elderly. We also organize community dining events to promote social interaction and a sense of belonging.

Support Us

Medical Aid

There are 91 elders (25 female and 66 male) benefiting from the health program of Asegedech Asfaw AARO.

Our medical program includes regular health check-ups, access to medications, and partnerships with local healthcare providers to address chronic conditions and emergencies. We also offer health education to promote wellness and prevention.
