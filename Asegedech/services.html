Shelter Aid

Safe shelter & clothing support

In our facility 91 elders (66 male and 25 female) receive full shelter and clothing aid. We also provide personal cleaning materials for dignity and comfort.

  • Clean rooms with warm bedding
  • Laundry, hygiene kits, and clothing
  • 24/7 on-site support and security
Food Aid

Nutritious meals, every day

We provide three meals per day for 91 residents (66 male and 25 female) and an additional 20 elders (17 male and 3 female) who live outside the facility.

  • Balanced menus tailored to health needs
  • Fresh ingredients and cultural dishes
  • Celebration meals for holidays and events
Medical Aid

Health support & wellness

There are 91 elders (25 female and 66 male) benefiting from the health program of Asegedech Asfaw AARO. We coordinate checkups, medication, and referrals.

  • Regular vital checks & medication support
  • First aid & emergency response coordination
  • Light physiotherapy & recreation

Common Questions

Who can receive services?

Elders in need, prioritizing those without family or stable income. Intake is coordinated with local administrators and community leaders.

How can I refer someone?

Call us at +251 991 586 162 or email contact@asegedechaged.com with basic details; we’ll schedule an assessment.
